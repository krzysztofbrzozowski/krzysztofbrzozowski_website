Cookies

My website, like almost all websites of that time, uses cookies to improve the quality of pages content.

I'm using cookies for the purposes:

  • Ensure the proper functioning of the website,
  • Improve the security and speed of the website,
  • Improvements to user functions,
  • Use of analytical tools,
  • Use of marketing tools,

During the first visit to the website, information about the use of cookies is displayed. Accept and close this information means, that you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with the provisions of this privacy policy.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by deleting cookies. The user has the option to configure his web browser in such a way as to prevent the storage of cookies on their end devices. Disabling cookies may cause difficulties while using the website.

Analysis and statistics

I also use cookies to track website statistics such as the number of visitors, time spent on the website, and visited pages, browser type and type of operating system used to display the page, etc. For this purpose, I use the Google Analytics tool. The use of the Google Analytics tool is related to the implementation of the tracking script (the so-called tracking code) provided by by google in the code of my site. This script is based on cookies but may also use other tracking technologies.
